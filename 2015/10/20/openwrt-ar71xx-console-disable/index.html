Disable console in OpenWRT ar71xx platform

Normally, to diable the console for OpenWRT, just follow:
http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/recipes/terminate.console.on.serial.
However, it doesn’t work well on ar71xx platform. We have to do it manually.

  1. Edit target/linux/ar71xx/image/Makefile.
  2. Replace “$(if $(1),board=$(1) )$(if $(2),console=$(2)$(COMMA)$(3))” with “$(if $(1),board=$(1) )$(if $(2),console=null)”.
  3. Recompile.

References:
https://dev.openwrt.org/ticket/11243
